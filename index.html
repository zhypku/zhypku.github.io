Hello, this is Hanyu.

I am Hanyu Zhao (赵汉宇), a 4th-year Ph.D. candidate at the Distributed Systems Lab of Peking University, supervised by Prof. Yafei Dai and Prof. Zhi Yang.
My research interests are in distributed systems, cloud computing, and systems for machine learning. My recent focus is on cluster-scale machine learning systems, and the classic distributed-systems challenges in this new context, e.g., resource scheduling, data storage. I also study distributed consensus protocols and data consistency.
In my leisure life I like workout (jogging/climbing/gym), taking photos (the banner is a recent one), and singing songs. Join me if interested! I used to be a member of the Debate Team of Wuhan University, one of the best Mandarin debate teams in the world :)
